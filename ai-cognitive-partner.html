Vol. IV · Cognitive partner

Thinking,
with help.

Most academic writing about AI is panicked or evangelical. Both miss what is actually happening. Careful, methodologically literate work with AI changes the shape of what we can think about, and what we can teach.

I work with Claude as a genuine cognitive partner. Daily, structured collaboration across research, writing, course design, and data analysis. Not an oracle. A colleague pretending to be a calculator.

I

The tool frame is outdated

Current models reason through novel problems, change strategy when stuck, and recognise when they are wrong. Distributed cognition (Hutchins), extended mind (Clark and Chalmers), and stigmergy (Heylighen) describe what is happening better than "tool use" does.

II

Augmentation mode

Anthropic's AI Fluency framework distinguishes automation, augmentation, and agency. Augmentation means working alongside the model, sparring with arguments, stress-testing reasoning. It produces more thinking, not less.

III

Course design as co-design

Three Fall 2026 courses at Nazarbayev University, with Claude integrated into the epistemology of each course. Claude writes its own positionality statements and students can push back on it.
