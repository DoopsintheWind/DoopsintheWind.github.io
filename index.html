§ Synopsis

I study how people make meaning of contested things. My current work is in Kazakhstan — a survey experiment on securitisation framing and law enforcement support, and a grounded theory study of heteronormative accountability and public attitudes toward sexual and gender minorities.

I'm also building a practice around AI-augmented social science - AI as a genuine cognitive partner in research and teaching. The field is wide open, and most of the interesting questions haven't been asked yet.
