Scenario

ASML and the Lithography Question

ASML, the Dutch manufacturer of extreme ultraviolet lithography machines, is the sole global supplier of the equipment needed to produce advanced chips below 7nm. China has placed orders worth $4.2 billion. The Netherlands has imposed partial export restrictions under US pressure, but China is lobbying The Hague directly and offering trade concessions across European markets. ASML's CEO has publicly warned that restrictions will cost European jobs and push China toward developing indigenous alternatives faster. You must decide the US position.
