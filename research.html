Field notebook — the specimens so far

I study how people interpret contested things: what they take to be true, who they trust and why, and how they make sense of the choices they're given. The work runs across meaning-making in authoritarian politics, the management of visible and invisible lives, and what actually moves attitudes when institutions try to persuade. A fourth strand is just beginning: AI in higher education, starting with fieldwork done with AI as a working partner.
