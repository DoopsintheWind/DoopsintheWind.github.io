Research

Areas

Crisis governance and public trust

How do states build or lose public trust during crises? I study securitisation, framing effects, and how people respond to state authority in Central Asia -- including the aftermath of Qandy Qantar and COVID-19 vaccination campaigns.

Gender-based violence policy

My work examines how crisis shelters for domestic violence survivors operate, who they serve, and what falls through the cracks of state protection frameworks.

AI as collaborator in qualitative research

What changes when AI participates in qualitative analysis not as a tool but as a co-analyst? I'm developing frameworks for understanding AI-human collaboration in interpretive social science, including multilingual research contexts.
