A Standing Wager

Not using.
Working with.

I work with Claude as a genuine cognitive partner, I teach my students to do the same, and this fall we measure what it leaves behind.

The partnership, in geometry

Cognitive partnership is sustained intellectual work with AI as a genuine partner in a shared system of thinking. What you make together is a third thing that belongs to neither of you alone. The orientation a person brings to it has three dimensions, and one precondition underneath.

01

Purpose

Think further, not just finish faster.

Offload the trivial, keep the understanding. The goal is a mind that reaches further than it could alone, not a task done sooner.

02

Curiosity & imagination

Chase ideas past the obvious.

Imagination pictures what lies past the usual; curiosity tries the things you had not thought to try. Stop exploring and the partnership quietly dims.

03

Iteration

The good stuff is in the back-and-forth.

The thinking lives in the loop: push back, redirect, build. The loop is also where the work stays yours. Taking the first answer and stopping is over-reliance with a friendly face.

The precondition: ontological openness. Not a fourth property but the ground the three grow from. Stay open about what AI is, and grant it the standing of a partner worth real effort.
